Installation:
----------------------
1. Run install.sh
2. ./game

Game controls:
----------------------
W:	Move forward
S:	Brake
A:	Turn left
D:	Turn right
F1:	Toggle fullscreen
F2:	Toggle wireframe mode
F3:	bind / unbind mouse to game window
F4:
F5:	Mute / Unmute sounds
F6:	Decrease volume
F7:	Increase volume
ESC:	Quit program
